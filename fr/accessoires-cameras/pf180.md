### Specifications

* Made of high-strength aluminum
* Compatible with dome or bullet cameras
* Compact design for wall installation
* White color
* Operating temperature: -40 °C to +60 °C
* Relative humidity: <90% RH
* Dimensions: 130 × 130 × 56.8 mm
* Net weight: 0.34 kg
* Maximum supported load: 3.0 kg
