# VESTA-482

## Adjustable mounting bracket for VESTA outdoor detectors VESTA-460 and VESTA-479

<figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/image (1316).png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Specifications

* Adjustable mounting bracket for VESTA outdoor detectors
* Allows a more precise and flexible installation
* Horizontal adjustment up to 90°
* Vertical adjustment up to 15° downward
* Compatible with VESTA-460 and VESTA-479 detectors
* Suitable for outdoor installations
* Optimizes sensor positioning and detection angle.
