### &#x20;Main features

* VESTA Advanced Video Starlight IP PTZ dome
* 5MP@25ips
* Motorised lens 4.8\~98 mm with 20x optical zoom and 16x digital zoom
* IR illumination up to 100m
* Auto tracking, people counting, face and fire detection
* Built-in microphone and speaker
* MicroSD slot up to 256 GB
* IP66 protection
* PoE+ power supply

#### Technical Features

* VESTA Advanced Video PTZ IP dome
* 1/2.8" progressive scan CMOS sensor
* Maximum resolution of 5MP
* Starlight technology
* Minimum illumination of 0.001 lux colour at F1.5 with AGC on
* 0.0005 lux minimum illumination in black and white at F1.5 with AGC on
* Auto Day/Night (ICR), colour and B/W mode
* 16x digital zoom
* Motorised lens 4.8\~98 mm
* 20x optical zoom
* Auto F1.6-F3.9 aperture
* 60°\~3.59° horizontal field of view
* Working distance from 30mm to 1000mm
* Infrared illumination up to 100m

#### Video

* H.265, H.264 and MJPEG video compression
* H.265 Main Profile
* H.264 Base Line Profile, Main Profile and High Profile
* Video bitrate from 32 Kbps to 16 Mbps
* Stream 1: 5MP@25 fps
* Stream 1 Resolutions: 2960x1664, 2560x1440, 1920x1080, 1280x960 and 1280x720
* Stream 2: D1@25 ips
* Stream 3: D1@25 fps
* Stream 2 and Stream 3 resolution: 704x576

#### Positioner

* Continuous 360° horizontal rotation
* 0.1°\~100°/s horizontal speed
* 0°\~93° vertical rotation
* 0.1°\~50°/s vertical speed
* 255 presets
* 6 rounds with up to 18 presets per round
* 4 patterns with total recording time no less than 10 min
* Recovery after power loss
* IR intensity automatically adjusted according to zoom ratio

#### Features

* Runner mode
* Adjustment of saturation, brightness, contrast, sharpness from client or browser
* Backlight compensation
* Auto exposure mode, aperture priority, shutter priority, and manual exposure mode
* Autofocus, single focus and manual focus
* Area exposure and focus
* Defog
* EIS
* 3D noise reduction
* 24-bit BMP image overlay with optional region
* 1 fixed ROI region for each stream
* AI-ISP
* Dark Killer Image Quality

#### Intelligent functions

* SMD
* Perimeter protection
* Auto tracking
* People counting
* Face detection
* Fire detection
* People and vehicle metadata

#### Audio

* Integrated microphone
* Built-in speaker
* G.711alaw, G.711ulaw, G.722.1, G.726, MP2L2 and PCM audio compression
* 64 Kbps audio bitrate in G.711
* 16 Kbps audio bitrate in G.722.1
* 16 Kbps audio bitrate in G.726
* 32\~192 Kbps audio bitrate in MP2L2
* 16\~64 Kbps audio bitrate in AAC

#### Storage

* Micro SD/SDHC/SDXC card slot up to 256 GB (card not included)
* NAS storage via NFS and SMB/CIFS

#### Network

* TCP/IP, ICMP, HTTP, HTTPS, FTP, DHCP, DNS, RTP, RTSP, RTCP, NTP, SMTP, SNMP and IPv6 protocols
* ONVIF Profile S and Profile G
* Private protocol
* API
* SDK

#### Power supply

* 12V DC 3A power supply
* PoE+

#### Environmental

* Operating temperature from -40 °C to 60 °C (-40 °F to 140 °F)
* Humidity up to 90%
* IP66 protection
* Surge protection
* TVS 6000V lightning protection

* Wall or ceiling installation
