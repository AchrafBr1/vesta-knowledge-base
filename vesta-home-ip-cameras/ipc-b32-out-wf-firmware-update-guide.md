# IPC-B32-OUT-WF Firmware Update Guide

This guide explains how to update the firmware of your **VESTA IPC-B32-OUT-WF camera** using **VESTA Tool**.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (586).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

{% hint style="info" %}
The firmware update fixes the following points:

* Improves **WiFi scanning**
* Enables **Human Detection activation from SmartHomeSec APP**&#x20;

The update process only takes a few minutes.
{% endhint %}

***

### Before You Start

Make sure you have:

* A **PC with** [**VESTA Tool installed**](https://vesta-guide.gitbook.io/vesta-guide/softwares-and-tools/vesta-config-tool)
* The **firmware file** provided in this guide

{% file src="../.gitbook/assets/Vesta_Home_Eng_P_SSV6355_V2.801.00BY001.0.R.260309 (1).bin" %}

* Your camera **powered on and connected to the network**
* The **Verification Code** (printed on the label of the camera)

***

## Update in 3 Simple Steps

### Step 1 — Open VESTA Tool

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (587).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

1. Launch **VESTA Tool** on your computer.
2. Go to **Search Settings**.
3. Enter the **Verification Code** shown on the camera label.
4. Click **Search** to detect the camera on your network.

***

### Step 2 — Open the Upgrade Section

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (588).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

1. Select your **IPC-B32-OUT-WF** camera from the device list.
2. Click **Upgrade** in the VESTA Tool interface.

***

### Step 3 — Upload the Firmware

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (589).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

1. Click **Browse / Select File**.
2. Choose the **firmware file (.bin)** provided in this guide.
3. Click **Upgrade**.

The update process will start automatically.

***

### Important Notes

* Do **not disconnect power** during the upgrade.
* The camera may **restart automatically** once the update is complete.
* The upgrade typically takes **2–3 minutes**.

***

### After the Update

Once the update is finished, your camera will include:

* Improved **WiFi network scanning**
* **Human Detection activation** support

{% hint style="success" icon="circle-info" %}
Its recomended to factory default de IP cam using its button after the update.&#x20;
{% endhint %}
