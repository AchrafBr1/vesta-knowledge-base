# SW016

<figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/image (1360).png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

#### Main features

* VESTA cloud-managed PoE switch
* 16 Gigabit PoE ports and 2 Gigabit cascade ports
* 1 Gigabit SFP interface
* PoE power of 30W per port
* 240W internal power supply
* Manageable via VESTA Advanced APP or VSS local software.

#### Technical features

* VESTA cloud-managed PoE switch
* 16 RJ45 1000Mbps PoE ports
* 2 RJ45 1000Mbps cascading ports
* 1 SFP 1000Mbps interface
* 38G bandwidth
* 28,272Mpps forwarding capacity
* 2K address MAC table
* AC 100\~240V input with 240W power supply
* 1000Base-T transmission with 4 pairs U/UTP or STP Cat5e or above up to 100m
* 100Base-TX transmission with 2 pairs U/UTP or STP Cat5 or higher up to 100m (100ft)
* 10Base-TX transmission with 2 pairs U/UTP or STP Cat5e or higher up to 250 m (820 ft)
* Compatible with IEEE 802.3af/at PoE equipment
* Compatible with certified non-standard PoE equipment

#### Functionalities

* Manageable through Vesta Advanced APP or local VSS software
* Store and forward architecture
* Automatic learning and ageing of MAC addresses
* IEEE 802.3x flow control in full duplex and backpressure in half duplex
* Port isolation configuration
* Port reboot
* Per-port PoE enablement and disablement
* Per-port rate limiting
* Long distance mode per port
* General equipment reset
* Equipment configuration reset
* Duplex configuration per port
* Firmware upgrade
* VLAN configuration

#### Intelligent functions

* Layered protection
* MAC/port binding
* DHCP snooping and protection against DHCP attacks
* End port security and isolation
* CPU protection
* ACL with port mirroring, port forwarding, flow limiting and relabeling QoS
* QoS with port priority, 802.1P and DSCP
* SP, WRR, SP + WRR and Equ scheduling algorithms

#### Network

* IEEE 802.3 Ethernet compliant
* IEEE 802.3u Fast Ethernet compliant
* IEEE 802.3i 10BASE-T Ethernet Compliant
* IEEE 802.3ab 1000BASE-T Gigabit Ethernet compliant
* IEEE 802.3z 1000Base-X SFP interface support
* IEEE 802.3af compliant
* IEEE 802.3at compliant
* IEEE 802.3x compliant
* 10Mbps half duplex and 20Mbps full duplex Ethernet speeds
* 100Mbps half duplex and 200Mbps full duplex Fast Ethernet speed
* Gigabit Ethernet 2000Mbps full duplex speed
* Cloud-based display of serial number, model, firmware version, real-time IP, real-time per-port power, LINK status, duplex, upstream/downstream speed and VLAN status
* Management via web interface
* Management via PC client
* Remote management via app

#### Power supply

* Up to 30W per port
* Overcurrent protection
* Overvoltage protection
* Short circuit protection
* Sequential start-up
* Soft start for high power PoE equipment

#### Environmental

* Operating temperature 0 °C to 40 °C
* Storage temperature -40 °C to 70 °C (-40 °C to 158 °F)
* Operating humidity 10 % to 90 % RH
* Storage humidity 5 % to 90 % RH
* Maximum working altitude 2000 m
* Maximum storage altitude 2000 m
* 4KV overvoltage protection

#### Physical characteristics

* 1U 19" rackmount installation
* Dimensions: 270 x 180 x 45 mm
* Weight: 1.35 kg
