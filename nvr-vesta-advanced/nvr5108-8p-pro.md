# NVR5108-8P-PRO

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (1363).png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Main features

* 8-channel IP NVR VESTA Advanced Video
* 96 Mbps bandwidth
* Compatible with IP cameras up to 8MP
* 4K HDMI output and VGA output
* Decoding up to 4 channels 1080P@30ips
* AI search
* Bidirectional audio
* 1 SATA bay up to 14 TB
* ONVIF compatible
* 8 PoE ports

#### Technical Features

* 8-channel NVR IP PoE VESTA Advanced Video
* 96 Mbps incoming bandwidth
* 96 Mbps outgoing bandwidth
* Supports up to 8 IP cameras up to 8MP
* 8 PoE ports
* Embedded Linux operating system
* Local GUI interface and web management

#### Video

* H.265 and H.264 video compression
* IP resolution up to 3840x2160
* 4-channel 1080P@30 fps or 1-channel 4K@30 fps decoding
* Synchronous playback of up to 8 channels
* HDMI output up to 3840x2160 resolutions
* VGA output with resolutions up to 1920x1080
* Display in 1, 4, 8 and 9 views

#### Functionalities

* Manual and scheduled recording in general, motion detection, alarm and intelligent modes
* AI search by human, vehicle and non-motorised vehicle classification
* Backup via USB devices
* Playback with pause, stop, fast forward, rewind, frame-by-frame playback, full screen, cropping, partial enlargement, and audio triggering
* JPEG image capture
* Access from Android and iOS
* P2P and push alarms in app
* IE8 browser or higher

#### Intelligent functions

* 8-channel camera analytics with perimeter protection
* 8-channel camera analytics with face detection
* 8-channel camera analytics with SMD

#### Audio

* 1 RCA audio input
* AI search
* Bidirectional audio
* 1 SATA bay up to 14 TB
* ONVIF compatible
* 8 PoE ports

#### Storage

* 1 SATA bay
* 14 TB maximum capacity

#### Network

* 1 RJ45 10/100M network interface
* Protocols: HTTP, TCP/IP, IPv4, UPnP, SNMP, RTSP, UDP, SMTP, NTP, DHCP, DNS, IP filtering, DDNS, FTP, P2P and Auto register
* ONVIF and SDK

#### Power supply

* 48 V DC power supply, 1.875 A
* 7 W power consumption without HDD

#### Environmental

* Operating temperature -10 °C to +55 °C (-10 °C to +55 °C)
* Operating humidity from 10% to 90%.

#### Physical characteristics

* Mini 1U form factor
* Desktop installation
* Dimensions: 215 x 235 x 45 mm
* Packing dimensions: 410 x 293 x 60 mm
* Weight: 1.7 kg without HDD

#### Certifications and standards

* CE and FCC markings
* NDAA: equipment compliant with the requirements of the National Defence Authorisation Act (Section 889)
