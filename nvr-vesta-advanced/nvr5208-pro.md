# NVR5208-PRO

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (1365).png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Main features

* 8-channel IP NVR VESTA Advanced Video
* 96 Mbps bandwidth
* Compatible with IP cameras up to 8MP
* 4K HDMI, VGA and CVBS output
* Decoding up to 4 channels 1080P@30ips or 1 channel 4K@30ips
* AI search
* Bidirectional audio
* 8 alarm inputs and 4 alarm outputs
* 2 SATA bays up to 14 TB
* Onvif compatible
* NDAA certification

#### Technical Features

* VESTA Advanced Video IP NVR Recorder with 8 channels
* 96 Mbps incoming bandwidth
* 96 Mbps outgoing bandwidth
* Supports up to 8 IP cameras up to 8MP
* Embedded Linux operating system
* Local GUI interface and web management

#### Video

* H.265 and H.264 video compression
* IP resolution up to 3840x2160
* 4-channel 1080P@30 fps or 1-channel 4K@30 fps decoding
* Synchronous playback of up to 8 channels
* HDMI output up to 3840x2160 resolutions
* VGA output with resolutions up to 1920x1080
* CVBS output
* Display in 1, 4, 8 and 9 views

#### Features

* Manual and scheduled recording in general, motion detection, alarm and intelligent modes
* AI search by human, vehicle and non-motorised vehicle classification
* Backup via USB devices
* Playback with pause, stop, fast forward, rewind, frame-by-frame playback, full screen, backup, partial enlargement, and audio triggering
* JPEG image capture
* Access from Android and iOS
* IE8 browser or higher

#### Intelligent functions

* 8-channel camera analytics with perimeter protection
* 8-channel camera analytics with face detection
* 8-channel camera analytics with SMD

#### Audio

* 1 RCA audio input
* 1 RCA audio output
* Bi-directional audio
* G.711A audio compression
* G.711A, G.711U and G.726 audio decoding
* 8 Kbps audio bitrate (16 bit)

#### Storage

* 2 SATA bays
* Maximum capacity of 14 TB

#### Alarms

* 8 alarm inputs
* 4 alarm outputs
* Alarm link with recording, snapshot, buzzer, logging, preset and e-mail

#### Communication interface

* 1 RS485 interface

#### Network

* 1 RJ45 1000M network interface
* Protocols: HTTP, TCP/IP, IPv4, UPnP, SNMP, RTSP, UDP, SMTP, NTP, DHCP, DNS, IP filter, DDNS, FTP, P2P and Auto register
* ONVIF and SDK

#### Power supply

* 12 V DC 3 A power supply
* 7 W power consumption without HDD

#### Environmental

* Operating temperature -10 °C to +55 °C (-10 °C to +55 °C)
* Working humidity from 10% to 90%.

#### Physical characteristics

* 1U form factor
* Desktop installation
* Dimensions: 360 x 262 x 48 mm
* Packing dimensions: 405 x 335 x 120 mm
* Weight: 2.3 kg without HDD

#### Certifications and standards

* CE and FCC markings
* NDAA: equipment compliant with the requirements of the National Defence Authorisation Act (Section 889)
