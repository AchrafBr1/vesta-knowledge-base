# VFA150

## VESTA pole mounting bracket

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (467).png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Specifications

* VESTA pole mounting bracket
* Compatible with junction boxes, wall brackets and power boxes
* Designed for Ø80 mm to Ø150 mm poles
* Load capacity: 7 kg
* Made of SECC steel with SUS304 stainless steel clamps
* Color: white
* Dimensions: 130.4 × 45 × 170 mm (130.4 × 45 × 170 mm)
* Net weight: 1.09 kg
* Gross weight: 1.28 kg
