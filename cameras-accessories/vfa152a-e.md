* VESTA pole mounting bracket
* Compatible with poles from Ø80 mm to Ø150 mm

* Made of aluminium alloy with SUS304 stainless steel clamps
* White color
* Pole installation (Ø80\~150 mm)
* Dimensions: 125.6 × 114 × 20 mm
* Net weight: 0.26 kg
* Gross weight: 0.36 kg
