# VESTA-238

## Battery in pack of 6 AA NI-MH batteries.

* Compatible with **VESTA-046N** (HSGW-G8-2G-F1-868-ZW-DT-18) and **VESTA-047N** (HSGW-G8-4G-F1-868-ZW-DT-18) control panels.
* 1100 mAh capacity
* Battery consisting of 6-pack of AA NI-MH batteries
